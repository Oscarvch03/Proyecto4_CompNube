FIFA 22 is a soccer video game developed by EA Romania and EA Vancouver, and published by EA Sports. It is available for PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, Xbox Series X / S, Xbox One, Microsoft Windows, Google Stadia, and Nintendo Switch. It is the twenty-ninth installment in the FIFA series and was released on October 1, 2021 globally.

The first trailer of the game was presented on July 11, 2021 and shows the new HyperMotion technology, which according to EA will provide a more realistic experience, in which the movements of several players, more organic dynamics, new animations were captured. much more realistic taken from thousands of frames, under EA's machine learning algorithm. These innovations are only available for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X and Series S.

Unlike previous games that had different editions with more content, FIFA 22 will only have two editions: Ultimate and Standard. The Ultimate edition grants the user 4 days early access to the game, along with extra benefits not available in the standard edition, with a respective value of $ 60 or $ 70 (standard edition) and $ 100 (Ultimate edition).
